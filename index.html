Studio
Improvise

An interior architecture and design practice based in Athens, Greece, founded in 2016 by Daphne Kala.

The word improvisation derives from the Latin in promptu — "in readiness". To improvise design is to invent it while performing it: a process demanding deep understanding of art, architecture, craftsmanship, nature, and human desire.

We work with clients to mould their needs and desires into physical spaces. We value collaborations, appreciate craftsmanship, and are always evolving.

Daphne graduated from the Glasgow School of Art with a Master's in Interior Design in 2015. She is currently based in Athens.
